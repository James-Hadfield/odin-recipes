STEPS

  1. Boil the potatoes;
  2. Pre-heat the oven;
  3. Saute the vegetables;
  4. Add the ground beef, then the Worcestershire sauce and broth;
  5. Mash the cooked potatoes;
  6. Layer the meat mixture and mashed potatoes in a casserole dish; and
    7. Bake in oven until browned (approx. 30 mins at 180 degrees.)
